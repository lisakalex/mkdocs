## Key Responsibilities

* Supervise front-of-house operations, including service preparation, food service, stock control, and menu coordination.
* Lead, motivate, and support Food Service Assistants and temporary staff to maintain high service standards.
* Assist the FOH Manager with daily operations, events, and service delivery.
* Ensure full compliance with Sodexo policies, health and safety regulations, and food hygiene standards.
* Build and maintain strong client relationships to support long-term partnerships.
* induct, train, and develop team members, addressing performance issues when required.
* Prepare staff schedules in line with operational and contractual requirements.
* Support initiatives to increase revenue in catering areas.
* Maintain accurate administrative records, reports, and documentation.
* Conduct risk assessments and maintain compliance documentation.
* Deliver staff inductions, performance reviews, coaching, and team briefings.
* Monitor stock levels, stock rotation, and accuracy of inventory counts.
* Ensure high standards of cleanliness, hygiene, and professional presentation.
* Attend meetings and training sessions and deputise for the FOH Manager when required.
