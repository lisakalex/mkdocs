# Stark company history

**Stark Software International Limited (Stark UK)** has a clear, evolution-focused history in the energy sector,
spanning over **45 years** as of 2026. Here's a concise summary you can reference or weave into conversation during your
interview—it shows you've done your homework and can tie into topics like innovation, growth, data expertise, and the
recent changes that make the Platform Engineer role timely.

### Founding and Early Years (1981–1990s)

- **Founded in 1981** by **Angela Hauser**, **Johanna Stark**, and **Howard Stark** (family roots, with Joel Stark now
  involved in leadership).
- The spark: Howard Stark's idea to **automate electricity tariff calculations** using early personal
  computers—pioneering software for energy data processing when most businesses handled it manually.
- Started as a **pure-play software development company** with deep domain expertise in energy (e.g., utilities,
  tariffs, billing).
- Quickly became known for innovating how businesses interact with energy data, setting the foundation for "Day+1"
  accurate data insights decades before it was common.

### Evolution Phases (2000s–2020s)

The company describes its growth in three main phases:

1. **First phase** — Focused on software-only solutions and building energy domain knowledge.
2. **Second phase** — Expanded into **direct data collection** from meters, processing, assurance, and combining it with
   powerful analytics (moving beyond pure software to end-to-end data services).
3. **Third phase** (ongoing) — Core data + analytics remains central, but they've added deeper capabilities like:
    - **Stark Charge** (EV charging infrastructure).
    - **Zero** platforms (Net Zero/decarbonization tools).
    - Broader metering, infrastructure, and hardware services.

- Over 40+ years, the mission has stayed consistent: Help clients **identify energy waste**, **save costs**, and *
  *reduce carbon emissions** through data, analytics, and infrastructure—positioning Stark as a "Tech For Good" player
  in the UK's energy transition.

### Recent Milestones (2020s Growth Phase)

- **Private equity backing**: Received investment from **BGF** (Business Growth Fund) around 2021 to fuel expansion.
- **Strategic long-term investment from Macquarie Capital** (announced January 2025) — This supports organic growth and
  acquisitions, with the Stark family retaining majority control.
- **Major acquisition**: In August 2025, Stark signed an agreement to acquire **Siemens' UK Energy Services business** (
  a non-core divestment by Siemens).
    - Focus: Engineering excellence in metering, installation, maintenance, and infrastructure.
    - Impact: Doubled the engineering team to **>250 engineers**, expanded field capabilities, and strengthened position
      in areas like Market-wide Half Hourly Settlement (MHHS) reforms and Net Zero goals.
    - Legal completion expected **early 2026** (so as of Feb 2026, integration is likely underway or recently
      finalized—perfect tie-in for why they're hiring the first dedicated Platform Engineer to standardize tooling
      across a larger, evolving team).
